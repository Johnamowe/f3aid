Welcome to Feminine, Family and Foundation Aid, where every woman counts!

F3AID is a not-for-profit, non-governmental women’s rights organization dedicated to the civil, political, economic and social rights of all women and girls. Registered in Nigeria in 2016, it was formed by a group of experts with extensive professional experience in the fields of gender and development. Our works strive to promote and defend gender equality globally by raising awareness, lobbying and advocating gender issues. F3AID aspires to contribute to global efforts to address gender disparity. We believe in a world where girls and women have the right to live in accordance with human dignity.

F3AID programs implement and support social work services to help individual survivors of gender based violence, economic empowerment activities to support survivors of violence and women and girls at-risk of violence, community education and mobilization projects around gender based violence, training and capacity-building for women and girls’ groups, coordination of humanitarian services, and advocacy efforts to advance laws preventing violence against women and girls, and the enforcement of policies ensuring survivors’ access to care and legal justice.

OUR VISION

F3AID envisions a world where women and girls are not marginalized and or discriminated against in their various societies and they can live a violence free life in dignity. We envision a world in which all women and girls can exercise their human rights to access the information, resources, and services they need to enhance and protect themselves and achieve their full potential; make informed decisions about their rights, free of discrimination, coercion, and violence; and participate in and assume leadership of civil society movements to advance their rights.

OUR MISSION

It is the mission of F3AID to raise awareness and educate others about global issues concerning the rights of women and girls by advocating for change at local, national and international levels with regards to the rights of women and girls. Also ensure women emancipation to effect deep sustainable change for women’s rights which requires women’s collective action and power.

OUR AIM

  • To improve gender equality and equity through advocacy and lobbying
  • To develop the organizational and human capacities of various actors to effectively deal with issues of gender equality and equity
  • To meet the safety, health, psychosocial and justice needs of women and girls who are survivors of or vulnerable to gender based violence

OUR STRATEGIC OBJECTIVE

F3AID's objective is to support, advise, advocate and empower women in the face of all forms of gender based violence and be their voice when needed, while working to end all gender based violence in our generation and gain equality for women and girls through:

  • Protection - by influencing policy change and key decision makers.
  • Prevention - through raising awareness and education.
  • Provision - of the services and support needed to help survivors of gender based violence.
  • Participation - by sharing good practice both locally and internationally on how to protect women and girls from gender based violence.

OUR CORE VALUES

  • Synergy across disciplines and geographies, within the organization and with our partners.
  • Passion driven by a personal commitment to make a positive difference.
  • Accountability for our work, measuring, reporting and continually improving all that we do.
  • Respect for diversity and cultural differences.
  • Innovation to meet the evolving needs of our beneficiaries, funders and partners.
  • Commitment to excellence assured by the highest ethical, quality, operational performance and scientific standards.
